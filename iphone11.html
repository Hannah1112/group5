Description

Apple has officially launched a trio of iPhone 11 super products, in which the 64GB iPhone 11 version has the cheapest price but is still upgraded as strongly as the iPhone XR launched before.

Powerful upgrade of the camera

Speaking of upgrades, the camera is the most improved point on the new iPhone generation


If before the iPhone XR had only one camera, now with the iPhone 11 we will have up to two cameras on the back.

In addition to the main camera, which still has a resolution of 12 MP, we will have an extra wide-angle camera with the same resolution.

According to Apple, switching between the two lenses will not change the color of the image.

This is something that is considered a turning point because Android smartphones with many cameras today will often have color deviations when switching between lenses, which is quite uncomfortable for users.

Besides, with the iPhone 11, this will be the first time Apple has equipped its iPhone with night photography capabilities.

According to experience, this feature works very effectively, especially in extremely low-light environments.

Activating the night mode will be decided by the iPhone itself, just pick up the camera and shoot, very simple.

This year Apple has also upgraded the front camera resolution to 12 MP instead of 7 MP like the previous generation. The front camera also has a smart feature, when you rotate the phone horizontally it will automatically activate the wide-angle selfie mode so you can take photos with more people.

In addition, Apple also introduced slow motion video recording for the front camera, something Apple has never equipped with previous iPhones.

World leading powerful performance

Every time a new iPhone is released, Apple gives users an experience of "unprecedented" performance.

On the new iPhone 11, Apple upgraded its chip to the very powerful Apple A13 Bionic generation.

This iPhone is also upgraded to 4 GB RAM instead of 3 GB like the previous generation.

At the above configuration level, the phone plays well with today's popular games in a smooth and stable way. Every operation on the new iPhone also gives a fast response speed that you will almost not feel the lag even after using it for a long time.

The iOS 15 version (December 2021) that comes with this iPhone is also equipped with more features to help you use your iPhone more efficiently.

Face ID on iPhone 11 has also been improved to be able to recognize at more angles and respond faster.

Design changes in a positive direction

We will have a finished glass back and Apple says that they have used the most durable glass ever for this iPhone.

The dual camera on the new iPhone is also redesigned and the good news is that it will be less protruding than on the previous iPhone XR.

The highlight of the large camera cluster on the back will help others easily recognize that you are using an iPhone 11 in your hand.

Apple's traditional apple logo has now been moved to the center of the large face instead of being skewed towards the top edge like previous iPhone models.

Best battery life ever

When it comes to iPhone 11 battery life, many people wish it had a battery as good as the iPhone XR (which has the best battery life of any modern iPhone).

So with the new iPhone, you can completely use the device for up to 2 days without worrying about the device running out of battery midway.

Of course, the device will also support fast charging technology, but you have to buy an external charger to be able to use this feature.
