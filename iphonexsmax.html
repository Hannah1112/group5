Description

Top performance with Apple A12 chip

iPhone Xs Max is equipped with Apple's brand new chip called Apple A12. The A12 Bionic chip is built on the first 7nm process that the company produces with 6 cores that excel in handling tasks and optimal energy saving.


Moreover, the iPhone phone also has a powerful graphics processor specifically designed by Apple, which greatly improves the graphics performance of the device.

Not only that, the machine is also integrated with artificial intelligence to help optimize hardware performance, so that your operations are processed more quickly.


High-quality, sturdy tempered glass and stainless steel bezel design

The iPhone Xs Max has a beautiful design with soft curves inherited from the previous iPhone.

However, the iPhone Xs Max has a large body equal to the size of the Plus series but contains a large screen up to 6.5 inches.

Thanks to that, you will have an extremely spacious experience space to enjoy high-quality movies to the fullest.

Apple's first 6.5-inch wide high-quality OLED display

With Super Retina technology combined with OLED panels on iPhone, it brings extremely vivid and sharp colors to every detail.

Besides, Apple also integrates HDR10 technology with 120 Hz touch frequency to help improve image quality and significantly smoother.

Owning a large screen gives you a lot of conveniences such as easily editing photos, watching movies, surfing the web, but it will be more difficult to move.

Dual camera integrated with artificial intelligence

Although it doesn't have great camera parameters (with only a 12 MP main lens and a 12 MP secondary lens), the iPhone Xs Max always shows its class in terms of top photography capabilities.

The machine is equipped with a quality image processing system that balances light, reduces noise, enhances exposure, and skin color so that it is most suitable and natural.

Along with that is the ability to adjust the impressive aperture from f/1.4 to f/16 right on the photo after taking a photo with portrait mode.

Not stopping there, the machine is also integrated with Smart HDR technology to help reproduce images and produce a picture with the best brightness.

In addition, iPhone Xs Max is also supported by artificial intelligence that automatically adjusts color, brightness and contrast to suit different objects.

Some premium features updated and added

Face ID has been improved by Apple in terms of security as well as for faster unlocking speed thanks to new algorithms.


Besides, the Animoji feature has also been updated with some new icons that look quite funny and lovely.

With the TrueDepth camera system, you can now create your own impressive photos with augmented reality technology.

In addition, the 2-way audio system on the new product has been refined by Apple for a wide sound range, more vivid sound or water and dust resistance has also been upgraded to IP 68 to ensure more safety for the device. .
